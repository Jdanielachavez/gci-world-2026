# GCI World 2026 April — University of Tokyo
### Global Consumer Intelligence Program · Matsuo-Iwasawa Laboratory

This repository holds my two final deliverables for the **GCI World 2026 April** program — a
competitive, project-based data-science course run by the **Matsuo-Iwasawa Laboratory at the
University of Tokyo**.

Both projects were designed, coded, analyzed, and written by me.

---

## Projects

### 🏈 Competition — NFL Draft Prediction
A leaderboard machine-learning competition: predict the probability that a college player is
drafted into the NFL, using physical and combine-performance data. My leakage-safe ensemble
reached **cross-validated ROC-AUC ≈ 0.85** (course baseline ≈ 0.78).

➡️ [`OMNICAMPUS U DE TOKIO/COMPETITION FINAL`](./OMNICAMPUS%20U%20DE%20TOKIO/COMPETITION%20FINAL)

### 📉 Final Assignment — Telecom Churn Reduction
An end-to-end business case — *Turning Customer Data into Retained Revenue* — a data-driven
churn-reduction proposal for an anonymized telecom ("Company A"), quantifying a
**$6.06M projected net benefit** from targeted retention.

➡️ [`OMNICAMPUS U DE TOKIO/FINAL ASSIGMENT FINAL`](./OMNICAMPUS%20U%20DE%20TOKIO/FINAL%20ASSIGMENT%20FINAL)
